What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a 24 hour coding spree where teams of up to 4 people create an app and fight for awesome prizes.

Does this hackathon cost money?

Absolutely not! This hackathon is for completely free.

Will there be food?

Of course! Food and snacks will be provided by us.

Who can participate in this hackathon?

Only high school students will be permitted to participate in this hackathon.
